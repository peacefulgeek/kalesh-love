## Idea 1: "The Sacred Glow" — Devotional Luminism

**Design Movement**: Inspired by Luminism (19th-century American landscape painting) crossed with sacred temple architecture. Think golden light streaming through ancient windows, the warm amber of candlelit meditation halls, the soft radiance of dawn over still water.

**Core Principles**:
1. **Golden warmth as the dominant emotional register** — warm ambers, soft golds, deep earth tones, cream, and touches of sacred saffron
2. **Light as a design element** — radial gradients, soft glows, luminous overlays that make the page feel lit from within
3. **Generous imagery** — large, immersive photographs that fill the viewport and create emotional resonance
4. **Organic flow** — no hard edges, no rigid grids; content flows like breath, like water

**Color Philosophy**: 
- Primary: Deep warm gold (#C8956C) — the color of aged brass temple bells
- Background: Warm cream (#FDF8F0) with subtle warm grain texture
- Text: Deep earth brown (#2D1B0E) — rich, readable, grounded
- Accent: Sacred saffron (#E8A849) — for highlights, hover states
- Secondary: Soft sage (#8B9A7B) — for balance, nature connection
- Dark sections: Deep twilight (#1A1520) with warm undertones

**Layout Paradigm**: Full-bleed immersive sections that alternate between large photographic moments and intimate reading spaces. The page breathes — wide open visual moments followed by narrow, focused text columns. Asymmetric placement of images that break the expected grid.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Soft radial light glows behind key headings — as if illuminated from behind
2. Subtle warm grain/noise texture on backgrounds — organic, not digital
3. Floating pull quotes with golden left borders and warm background washes

**Interaction Philosophy**: Gentle parallax on hero images. Content fades in with warmth — opacity + slight upward drift. Hover states are warm glows, not cold highlights. Links breathe with color shifts from earth to gold.

**Animation**: 
- Hero images have a slow, breathing scale animation (1.0 to 1.02 over 8s)
- Sections fade in with 800ms ease as you scroll — like dawn breaking
- Pull quotes slide in from the left with a warm glow trail
- Page transitions feel like turning pages in a sacred text

**Typography System**:
- Display: Playfair Display (warm, elegant serif with high contrast)
- Body: Source Serif 4 (readable, warm, humanist serif)
- Accent/Meta: Cormorant Garamond (ethereal, refined)
- Mono elements: None — this isn't a tech site

## Idea 3: "The Inner Temple" — Sacred Modernism

**Design Movement**: Inspired by Tadao Ando's sacred architecture crossed with illuminated manuscripts. Clean modern lines but filled with warm light, rich materials, and spiritual depth. Think concrete and gold leaf, minimalism with soul.

**Core Principles**:
1. **Sacred geometry meets modern clarity** — clean structure filled with warm, rich content
2. **Material richness** — gradients that feel like brushed gold, shadows that feel like candlelight
3. **Cinematic scale** — full-viewport hero moments with dramatic, soul-stirring imagery
4. **Intimate reading** — article pages feel like private meditation chambers

**Color Philosophy**:
- Primary: Brushed gold (#B8924A)
- Background: Warm ivory (#FFFBF5) to deep charcoal (#1C1917) for dark sections
- Text: Rich espresso (#2C1810) on light, warm cream (#FFF8EE) on dark
- Accent: Rose quartz (#C4918A) — heart energy
- Secondary: Deep teal (#2A6B6B) — depth, ocean, consciousness

**Layout Paradigm**: Cinematic full-bleed hero sections with text overlays, followed by centered reading columns with generous margins. Dark/light section alternation creates rhythm and drama. Images break out of containers to create visual surprise.

**Signature Elements**:
1. Gold gradient accents on headings and dividers
2. Soft warm shadows (not gray — tinted amber/brown)
3. Subtle background gradient shifts as you scroll — the page "warms" as you go deeper

**Typography System**:
- Display: Cormorant Garamond (ethereal, sacred feeling)
- Body: Source Serif 4 (warm, readable)
- Accent: Raleway (clean, modern contrast)

## CHOSEN APPROACH: Idea 1 — "The Sacred Glow" (Devotional Luminism)

This is the right choice because it puts WARMTH and LIGHT at the absolute center. The golden palette, luminous backgrounds, immersive photography, and temple-like atmosphere will make visitors feel held, inspired, and nourished. Every element radiates love and presence.

Key commitments:
- Warm gold/amber/cream palette — NO cold blues, NO sterile whites
- Large, immersive, soul-stirring imagery on EVERY page
- Soft radial glows and warm grain textures throughout
- Playfair Display + Source Serif 4 typography — warm, elegant, human
- Generous animations that feel like breathing, not clicking
- Dark sections with deep warm twilight tones for contrast and drama
- The site must feel like a sanctuary, not a document
